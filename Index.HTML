Steps

    1. Mold ground turkey into a patty 2. Place patty onto pan 3. than crack egg onto another pan to fry 4. Put the hashbrown into a oven to cook 5. (Optional) Toast the buns

Assembling The Brunch Burger

    1. Grab the bottom bun 2. Place the patty on the bun 3. Place a slice of cheese 4. Place the Hashbrown 5. Place the fried egg 6. Place The Top bun
Side Note

If you want to alter this in anyway go ahead and make your own brunch burger everyone has different taste
